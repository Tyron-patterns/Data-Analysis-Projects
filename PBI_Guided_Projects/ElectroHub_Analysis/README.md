# 📱 ElectroHub – Sales & Profit Analysis (Power BI)

This project analyzes sales, profit, and customer behavior for **ElectroHub**, a fictional retail chain selling electronic devices (e.g. smartphones, laptops, TVs, accessories) across multiple cities in India.

The dashboard was developed as part of a **guided Power BI learning project**, with the goal of practicing end-to-end data modeling, DAX measures, and business-oriented reporting.

---

## 📊 Project Overview

The report provides a comprehensive view of business performance, focusing on:

- Overall **sales, profit, and units sold**
- **Top and bottom products** by sales, profit, and volume
- **Sales trends over time**
- **Geographical performance** by city
- Impact of **promotions and discounts**
- Relationship between **sales and profit**

The emphasis is on building a clear analytical narrative rather than purely descriptive visuals.

---

## 🧱 Data Model

The dashboard follows a **star schema** structure:

- **Fact Table**
  - Fact Sales (orders, net sales, profit, discounts, units sold)

- **Dimension Tables**
  - Customers
  - Products
  - Promotions
  - Date (multiple date tables for flexible filtering)

This structure enables scalable filtering, time intelligence, and clean measure definitions.

---

## 📐 Key Measures & Analysis

Key DAX measures include:

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Number of Orders
- Units Sold
- Average Discount
- Sales vs Profit correlation
- Period-over-period comparisons using date slicers

The project focuses on understanding **relationships and trade-offs**, such as:
- High sales vs low profitability
- Discount intensity vs revenue impact
- Product volume vs margin contribution

---

This project is intended to demonstrate:

- Practical **Power BI modeling skills**

- Clear **business-focused dashboard design**
